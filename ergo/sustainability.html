Sustainability

We are here to help you establish and manage strategic energy and sustainability programmes across your portfolio to achieve your environmental, social, governance and resiliency goals. We provide Lifecycle assessment and carbon footprint calculation to reduce current standards.

Contact Us to Learn more about How Ergo Energy Can help you.

Step 1

Understand the company’s sustainability ambitions, translate those into goals and align all stakeholders on these goals.

Step 2

Develop governance mechanism around all the Environment, De-carbonization & ESG parameters.

Step 3

Manage the reporting of ESG parameters to stakeholders and management.

Step 4

Review environmental assessments and environmental management plans, covering a broad range of issues (e.g., energy, air quality, noise, ecology, water quality).

Step 5

Lead and guide the Business Units in development of environmental management plans and monitoring programs to meet ESG requirements.

Step 6

Working with various stakeholders towards ensuring formulation of the organization’s approach towards a robust sustainability strategy and program.

Step 7

Preparing ESG communication materials and helping top management to make effective communication to stakeholders.

Step 8

Support in the preparation and implementation of medium- and long-term Energy Management plans in line with Net Zero targets.
